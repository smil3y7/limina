**Tech stack**

Čisti vanilla — brez frameworkov, brez bundlerjev, brez odvisnosti razen Google Fonts. Vse teče v brskalniku.

- **HTML5** — single file, vse vgrajeno
- **CSS3** — custom properties (design tokens), `backdrop-filter`, `position: sticky/fixed`, CSS animations, `@keyframes`, media queries
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — ES6+, brez knjižnic
- **Web Audio API** — `AudioContext`, `OscillatorNode`, `GainNode`, `BiquadFilterNode`, `StereoPannerNode`, `AudioBufferSourceNode` za vse zvočne plasti
- **localStorage** — persistenca teme, jezika, HP check statusa
- **Google Fonts** — Cormorant Garamond (serif, display), DM Sans (sans-serif, UI)
